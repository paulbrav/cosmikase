## Adding Your Own Wallpapers

To add custom wallpapers to any theme:

1. Add image files to `themes/<theme-name>/backgrounds/`
2. Recommended naming: `1-theme-name.jpg`, `2-theme-name.png`, etc.
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, JPEG
4. Recommended resolution: 1920x1080 or higher

## Wallpaper Cycling

If your theme switcher supports it, wallpapers will cycle through the
numbered files in the backgrounds/ directory.
